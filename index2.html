The Squid Manifesto

01 // COUNT

Count Every Token

Track usage across every model, every prompt, every session. Know exactly where your tokens go — no surprises at the end of the month.

02 // UNIFY

One Workspace, All Models

Bring your paid subscriptions, free tiers, and local models into a single CLI. Switch providers without switching tools.

Context That Travels

Share context explicitly across models, topics, and sessions. No copy-paste. No repeating yourself. Just smart context reuse.
